* 1 Quart FRESH cream (Not Ultra Pasturized)*
* 1 Cup flour
* 1 Tablespoon sugar
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 3.5 cups whole milk scalded

Heat cream over medium heat for 15-20 minutes (do not boil).

When it starts to bubble slightly whisk in flour, sugar, salt.

Contniue cooking and beating until thickened.

Add hot milk about a cup at a time, and beat until smooth after each addition.

When all the milk has been added, continue cooking over medium heat while whipping for 3-4 minutes.

Strain through strainer.

Yield: Approx 1.75 Quarts 

Source: First Lutheran, at Host Fest 2024
